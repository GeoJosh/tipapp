Tip Northshore

Helping our community one service professional at a time.

The emergence of the COVID-19 pandemic has impacted communities across the globe. In the United States alone millions have been furloughed or lost their jobs due to the necessary closure of businesses. Our community is no exception to this outcome. Numerous community members are no longer able to recieve their income due to the closure of their employee's business with no end in sight.

Tip Northshore is a way to provide some direct relief to those impacted professionals right here in our own community. Tip Northshore is provided as a free service to the community and collects no fees nor personal data. This allows you provide maximum benefit to your fellow community members.

Want to help?

Helping out is easy. Click the Tip Northshore button and you will be given the information about a random impacted community member with a link to tip them directly.

Are you impacted?

If your job was impacted by business closures due to COVID-19 fill out an application to be added to the tipping pool immidiately.
